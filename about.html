I'm an interaction designer and researcher working in the field of media architecture and urban informatics. I hold a B.Sc. in Media Informatics from the University of Munich, Germany. In 2016, I was a visiting scholar at the Design Lab at University of Sydney, Australia conducting his master thesis project. Further, I worked on several media architecture installations for public art festivals, including Luminale in Frankfurt and Vivid Sydney.
