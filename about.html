I'm a programmer and maker, interaction designer and researcher. I hold a B.Sc. in Media Informatics from the University of Munich, Germany. I gained professional experience in Interface and User Experience Design for the Web, Mobile and Embedded Application Development for the Internet of Things, and Interactive Lighting Design.
In 2016, I was a visiting scholar at the Design Lab at University of Sydney (Australia) to conduct my master thesis project. My research interests include the development of prototyping methods and tools for complex design contexts beyond the screen. I am particularly interested in media architecture and urban interaction design. In this context, I worked on several light installations for public art festivals, including Luminale in Frankfurt and Vivid Sydney.
